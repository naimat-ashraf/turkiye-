About Izmir

Izmir is a vibrant coastal city on the Aegean Sea, known for its lively bazaars, ancient ruins, modern seaside promenade, and rich cultural heritage.

History of Izmir

3rd Century BC

Izmir, ancient Smyrna, flourished as a port city under Greeks.

133 BC

Became part of the Roman Empire, thriving in trade and culture.

14th Century

Integrated into the Ottoman Empire, developing as a coastal hub.

Present

Modern Izmir is a cosmopolitan city famous for festivals, cuisine, and seaside life.
