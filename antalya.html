About Antalya

Antalya is known for its turquoise beaches, ancient history, and vibrant coastal culture. It is one of Turkey’s most visited tourist destinations with a mix of modern and historic attractions.

History of Antalya

150 BC

Founded by King Attalus II of Pergamon.

133 BC

Became part of the Roman Empire.

1207

Conquered by the Seljuk Turks.

Present

A major global tourist hotspot known for beaches and resorts.
